# Bestiary

Average stats for common NPCs and creatures. The **Defense listed is always the unarmored
Defense**; bonuses from armor or shields are shown in parentheses. To make non-human NPCs (an
Orcish bandit, an Elfish mage), add the racial talents from `races-and-optional-rules.md`.

**Stat-block format:** Attributes (Warrior / Rogue / Mage), HP, Mana, Defense (+armor),
Skills, Talents, Spells, Trappings. Animals instead list their **Attacks** (the attribute each
uses, and damage). An attack marked with an asterisk (**\***) does **not** benefit from the
exploding-die rule.
