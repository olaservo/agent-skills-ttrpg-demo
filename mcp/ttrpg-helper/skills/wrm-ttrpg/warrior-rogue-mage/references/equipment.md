# Equipment

Each character starts with **250 silver pieces (SP)** to purchase equipment. **Every character
can wield every weapon.** Spellcasting characters may wear armor, but the armor's **Armor
Penalty (AP)** raises the mana cost of each spell cast (see `magic.md`).

### Weapon notes

- **Bow / Longbow:** a bundle of 10 arrows costs 2 SP. A **bow** can be used from horseback; a
  **longbow cannot**.
- **Crossbow:** takes one round to reload; a bundle of 10 bolts costs 2 SP. Can be used from
  horseback.
- **Dragon pistol / rifle:** firearms invented during the golden age of the Imperium. Reloading
  takes one round; 10 shots cost 4 SP. The rifle has longer range and more damage than the
  pistol.
- **Halberd:** has a reach of 2 yards.
- **Two-handed weapons:** swords, axes, maces, and warhammers exist in larger two-handed
  versions (2d6). Use the appropriate skill for each.
- **Unarmed:** fists cause 1d6/2 damage (rounded down); minimum damage caused is **1**.
- **Torch:** a lit or unlit torch is an improvised weapon (Blunt skill): 1d6-2 unlit, 1d6-1 lit;
  flammable enemies may be set ablaze.

| Padded cloth | 1 | 0 | 8 |

| Lamellar armor | 4 | 3 | 35 |
| Chain mail | 5 | 4 | 70 |
| Light plate armor | 6 | 5 | 90 |
| Heavy plate armor | 7 | 5 | 120 |
| Golem armor | 8 | see notes | N/A |
| Small shield | +1 | +2 | 5 |
| Large shield | +2 | +4 | 12 |
| Tower shield | +3 | +6 | 15 |

*Stats are for full suits of armor. Defense from armor/shields adds to the character's Defense;
Armor Penalty adds to spell mana costs.*

### Armor notes

- **Plate armor** must be fitted to the wearer. **Ill-fitted plate reduces all rolls by 2.**
- **Golem armor:** bulky armor of a bygone era; incredibly tough and grants **+1d6 to all weapon
  damage**, but **casting is impossible** while wearing it. Not available for sale. (Rumors tell
  of special golem armors that allow casting and are magic implements in themselves.)
- **Scale / Lamellar:** scale is small scales on a leather/cloth backing; lamellar needs no
  backing.
