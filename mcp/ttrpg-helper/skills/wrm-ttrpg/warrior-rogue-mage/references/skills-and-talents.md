# Skills & Talents

## Skills

Skills are **unranked** - a character either knows a skill or does not. A known, applicable
skill adds **+2** to a check using its attribute and lets the die explode on a 6. You cannot
take a skill whose attribute is ranked 0. Characters start with **three** skills; the GM may add
more skills to the list as needed.

| Skill | Attribute | Covers |
|---|---|---|
| Acrobatics | Rogue | Dancing, contortion, climbing, tightrope walking, tumbling |
| Alchemy | Mage | Creating and identifying potions and salves |
| Athletics | Warrior | Swimming, running, jumping |
| Awareness | Mage | A measure of the character's awareness of their surroundings |
| Axes | Warrior | Fighting with axes |
| Blunt | Warrior | All blunt weapons, including maces and staves |
| Bows | Rogue | Bows and crossbows |
| Daggers | Rogue | Daggers and knives |
| Driving | Warrior | Driving vehicles |
| Firearms | Rogue | The exotic firearms (dragon pistol, dragon rifle) |
| Herbalism | Mage | Plants, herbs, and their medical uses; can speed healing of the critically wounded |
| Lore | Mage | General knowledge |
| Polearms | Warrior | Polearms, spears, and lances |
| Riding | Warrior | Riding horses and other common mounts |
| Swords | Warrior | All kinds of swords, including two-handed ones |
| Thaumaturgy | Mage | Arcane spells and rituals (helpful but not required to cast) |
| Thievery | Rogue | The roguish arts: picking locks, picking pockets |
| Thrown | Rogue | Thrown weapons like throwing stars |
| Unarmed | Warrior | Unarmed fighting |

> Note: weapon skills are how the die explodes in combat. Attacking with a weapon whose skill
> you know lets your attack roll explode on a 6; attacking without the skill does not.

## Talents

Talents grant special abilities. You do **not** roll to use a talent - acquiring it grants the
ability. Characters start with **one** talent; some may be taken more than once. The GM may add
more talents.

| Talent | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Armored Caster** | Reduce your Armor Penalty by 2. May be taken more than once. |
| **Blood Mage** | You may use hit points instead of mana when casting spells - some or all of a spell's cost may be paid in HP. |
| **Champion** | Select a cause. Gain +2 on attack and damage rolls against enemies of that cause. May be taken more than once (different causes). |
| **Channeller** | Once per combat, add your Mage attribute level to your magic-attack damage. |
| **Craftsman** | You are trained in a craft (blacksmithing, carpentry, bowmaking, etc.). May be taken more than once. |
| **Dual Wielding** | Wield a weapon in your off-hand without penalty. Does **not** grant an extra attack (see Double Attack in `races-and-optional-rules.md`). |
| **Familiar** | You have a small animal companion (cat, falcon, etc.) that can perform simple tricks. |
| **Henchman** | A henchman follows you, carrying equipment and treasure and performing simple tasks. |
| **Hunter** | You can live off the land easily; given enough time you can feed a party of four. |
| **Leadership** | A talented leader who may command troops. |
| **Lucky Devil** | Reroll any one roll once per scene (or combat). |
| **Massive Attack** | Once per combat, add your Warrior attribute level to your melee-attack damage. |
| **Precise Shot** | Once per combat, add your Rogue attribute level to your ranged-attack damage. |
| **Sailor** | Trained in steering a boat or sailing ship; no penalties for fighting on a sea vessel. |
| **Sixth Sense** | Roll a die before any ambush or surprise; on a 4+ you are not surprised and may act first. |
| **Tough As Nails** | Damage you take from an individual attack is reduced by 2. |

### Racial talents

Non-human races grant additional talents (some are drawbacks for balance) - see
`races-and-optional-rules.md` for the full list (Exceptional Attribute, Weak, No Talent for
Magic, Natural Armor, Berserker, Outcast, Tinkerer, and the racial use of Sixth Sense, Hunter,
Lucky Devil, and Craftsman).
