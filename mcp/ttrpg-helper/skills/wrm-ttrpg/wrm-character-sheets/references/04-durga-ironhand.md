## Biography

Durga learned the forge in the under-tunnels of a mining city, where dwarven smiths are reckoned
among the best in the world, and where a bad weld can collapse a gallery onto a hundred miners. She
brought that same zero-tolerance for failure up into the daylight. In a fight she plants herself,
raises the shield, and simply does not fall over: with Defense 14 and Tough As Nails she shrugs off
the small stuff while the others do the clever things. She distrusts magic on principle - "if you
can't hammer it, dent it, or sell it, I don't trust it" - which is the dwarven way, and means the
party's mage gets a great deal of good-natured grief. Off the clock she is fixing everyone's gear,
usually without being asked and always while complaining about the quality of human steel.
