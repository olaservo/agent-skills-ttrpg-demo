## Biography

Pip grew up in a snug halfling town where the worst crime was an unlatched pantry - which she
robbed, repeatedly, for the thrill rather than the pies. Boredom and a knack for being somewhere
she shouldn't drove her out into the wide world of the big folk, where those same talents are
worth actual silver. She is cheerful, fast, and allergic to fair fights: her plan is always to be
up a wall or behind a corner with an arrow nocked before anyone knows she is there. With
Exceptional Attribute in Rogue she rarely fumbles the sneaky stuff, and Lucky Devil bails her out
when she does. Her one rule: never rob someone poorer than herself.
