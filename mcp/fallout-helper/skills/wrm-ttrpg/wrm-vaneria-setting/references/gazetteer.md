# Gazetteer of Vaneria

The seven named places of the fallen Imperium. Distances and a full map are left to the GM; the
relationships below are all the source specifies.

## Tukrael — the dead capital

The **former capital of the Imperium**, razed at the end of the civil war and now **overrun by
the undead**. No sane person dares explore the ruins of old Tukrael - which means untold Imperial
wonders may still lie hidden among them. (It sits close to the city-state of Bekel.)

## Vaikus — the civilized north

A city-state in the foothills of the **Dragontooth Mountains** in northern Vaneria, and the
**most civilized** of the Imperium's successors. Vaikus has a strong **caste system**: the Noble
and Warrior castes hold power while the lower castes provide the city's prosperity. Its elite
**Falcon Knights** are among the few military units that still carry **dragon pistols** as
sidearms. Vaikus's greatest rival is Cemimus.

## Joakalavi — the great trade city

A city in the **Central Desert**. Once an unimportant Imperial outpost, it has become the **most
important trade city on the continent**: caravans from every city-state meet and deal in this
beautiful city. **None but the Scorpion Guard are allowed to bear arms in Joakalavi.**

## Traevar — the magical academy

A relatively small city on the shores of **Lake Anytes**. It is known for the **Dark Spire**, the
**last remaining magical academy** from Imperial times.

## Cemimus — the ambitious rival

The greatest rival of Vaikus, and one of the nations that claim the Imperial throne - it is ruled
by a **direct descendant of Emperor Aurelius III**. Cemimus is known for its **ruthless mercenary
armies** and the **corruption** of its officials. It is currently **waging war against its
neighbor, Bekel**.

## Bekel — the mining city

A city-state south of Cemimus, close to the former Imperial capital of Tukrael. It is famed for
the **large ore deposits mined directly beneath the city**; a large portion of its miners **never
leave the underground tunnels**. **Bekelan engineers and blacksmiths are among the best in the
world.** Bekel is under attack from Cemimus.

## Chaetril — seat of the faith

One of the smaller city-states, in the **grasslands west of Vaikus**. Chaetril **exports
livestock and horses** to all the other city-states - its horses are second only to Imperial
Warhorses. It is also the seat of the **Patriarch of the Imperial Faith**, the major religion of
the continent, and its **cathedral is home to the Paladins**, an elite unit of church knights.
