# Warrior, Rogue & Mage skills — Attribution & Licensing

This folder (`wrm-ttrpg/`) is a collection of agent skills for **Warrior, Rogue & Mage
(WR&M)**, a free, rules-light fantasy tabletop RPG by **Michael Wolf**, published by
**Stargazer Games** (<https://www.stargazergames.eu/>). WR&M is offered free of charge and
released under open **Creative Commons** licenses — *not* the Open Game License.

These skills are an **unofficial fan project**. They are not produced or endorsed by Michael
Wolf or Stargazer Games. WR&M is free — please visit Stargazer Games to download the original
rulebook, the System Reference Document, and the supplements, and to support the author's work.
