## Biography

Aurelia took her vows with the Imperial Faith out of Chaetril, expecting a quiet life of
ministering to pilgrims. Then she watched a "miracle" in the grasslands turn out to be a buried
Imperial machine, and the cathedral sent her - quietly - to learn what else the old empire had left
lying around. She is the rare healer who does not hide behind the fighters: with a sword, leather,
and three points of Warrior she will stand in the second rank, patch the front line between swings,
and burn her own blood to keep them up when the mana runs out (Blood Mage). She is kind but not
naive, increasingly unsure whether the Faith she serves is preserving the Imperium's wonders or
hoarding them, and quietly keeping a list of the questions her superiors will not answer.
