## Biography

Lyrandel trained at Traevar's Dark Spire, the last magical academy of the old Imperium, where she
was a brilliant and impatient student - too impatient, in the end, to wait the decades her elders
said real mastery required. She left with a half-finished education, a stolen spellbook, and a
conviction that the Imperium's lost magic should be *used*, not catalogued. Physically she is
frail (four hit points and no armor), so she lives and dies by initiative and positioning: Sixth
Sense keeps her from being caught flat-footed, and Channeller lets her open a fight by detonating
one enemy outright. She treats the party's fighters less as comrades than as very reliable
furniture between her and anything with a sword.
