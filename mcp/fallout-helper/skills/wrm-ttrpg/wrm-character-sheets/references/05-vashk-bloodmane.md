## Talents

- **Massive Attack** (chosen) - once per combat, add Warrior to a melee damage roll.
- **Berserker** (racial) - may go berserk: **+2 Warrior and +2 to all damage**, and he ignores
  damage effects until the rage ends (tally it and apply it after). He must attack the nearest
  enemy, melee only; Mage drops to 0 (already is). To end the rage when enemies are down, make an
  Easy (DL 5) check with **unmodified Mage** - hard for him, so the rage tends to run its course.
- **Exceptional Attribute (Warrior)** (racial) - Warrior checks roll **2d6 keep highest**.
- **Outcast** (racial) - **-3** on social tests with members of other races.

## Biography

Vashk left his clan after refusing an order he judged dishonorable - among orcs, honor is not a
soft virtue but a hard law, and breaking with it made him an outcast twice over: shunned by his own
and feared by everyone else. He travels with a black wolf that likes him better than most people
do. In a fight he is terrifyingly simple: he picks the biggest threat, lets the rage take him, and
does not stop until it or he is down. The danger is the rage's tail end - when the enemies are dead
he often cannot calm himself, and the party has learned to give him room. Out of battle he is
quiet, scrupulously fair, and visibly tired of being treated as a monster by people he keeps
saving.
