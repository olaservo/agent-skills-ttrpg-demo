## Biography

Brannic was the third son of a minor house sworn to a northern city-state - too far down the
line to inherit, so he was given a sword and a horse and told to make a name. He earned it the
hard way, holding a bridge against a warlord's outriders until the levy could form up behind him.
He is unshowy, dependable, and physically the wall the rest of the party hides behind; his idea
of tactics is "get between the monster and the mage." He still wears his house colors, faded now,
and is privately certain the old Imperium could be rebuilt by better people than the ones
squabbling over its throne.
