# Setup - what the table needs

Load this when a new group is preparing a session.

## People

- **3-5 players** plus **1 gamemaster (GM)**. Each player picks one character; the GM runs everything else - NPCs, creatures, the world, and the rules.
- **Pre-generated characters** are recommended for first sessions. Fill them in pencil; details change as play progresses.
- **The GM reads the adventure first** so the players' choices get a real reaction.

## Dice

Two die types:

- **d20** - twenty-sided. At least **two per player** is ideal; a shared set works in a pinch. The dice pool never exceeds 5d20.
- **d6** used as **Combat Dice (CD)**. At least **four total** at the table. Modiphius sells custom CD with symbols on the faces; ordinary numbered d6s work using the lookup table on page 23 of the rulebook.

## Tokens for Action Points

- Anything countable: poker chips, beads, glass beads, bottle caps. Modiphius sells branded caps.
- Without tokens, track AP on paper - one player tallies the group pool, the GM tracks their own.

## Paper

- Spare sheets, a notebook, or digital notes for character notes, quest leads, and named NPCs.
- Pencil is preferred over pen because character details change.
